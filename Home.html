Photo of the Day

NASA's Astronomy Picture of the Day (APOD) showcases breathtaking images of space, featuring galaxies, nebulae, planets, and other cosmic wonders. Each photo is accompanied by a brief explanation from professional astronomers, making it a daily source of inspiration and discovery for space enthusiasts.

Near Earth Objects

Near Earth Objects by definition are asteroids or comets that orbit close to Earth within 1.3 Astronomical Units from the Sun.
